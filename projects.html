    Innovative Engineering Solutions
    Mass Engineering Consultancy Company

    Rehabilitation and redesign project of the Libyan Center for Oncology in Ain Zara, Tripoli.

    We have successfully completed a rehabilitation and redesign project for the Libyan Center for Oncology in Ain Zara, Tripoli. This undertaking posed a significant engineering challenge, requiring the restructuring and improvement of both the interior and exterior design of the center. Our specialized team assessed the needs and developed innovative solutions to enhance patient flow and provide a comfortable and safe treatment environment. Close collaboration with the center's team and leveraging the latest engineering technologies and practices ensured our success. Through our efforts, we have transformed the center into a modern and advanced facility that meets the needs of cancer treatment and delivers excellent patient care.

    Amwaj Project In Janzour

    In the Amwaj Janzur project, we provided comprehensive architectural design, structural engineering, and project management services. Our team created innovative designs, ensured structural stability, and managed the project efficiently. The result is a remarkable development that meets the client's vision and industry standards.

    Headquarters of the Libyan Islamic Bank.

    In the Libyan Islamic Bank Headquarters project, Mass Engineering Consultancy Company provided comprehensive architectural and engineering services to create a prestigious and functional headquarters. Our team collaborated closely with the bank to design and construct a modern facility that aligned with their vision and corporate identity. We oversaw various aspects of the project, including architectural design, interior design, structural engineering, mechanical systems, electrical systems, and project management. With meticulous attention to detail and a focus on quality, we successfully delivered a state-of-the-art headquarters that reflects the bank's prominence in the financial industry and provides a comfortable and efficient working environment.

    The Residential building Ben Ashour

    Libyan Islamic Bank Al-Zahraa Branch
    External And Interior

    In Libyan Islamic Bank Alzahra branch, Mass Engineering Consultancy Company provided comprehensive architectural, mechanical, and electrical works for the project. We were responsible for the design and execution of the branch, ensuring that it met the bank's requirements and standards. Our scope of work included the architectural design, interior design, and layout planning of the branch. We also handled the mechanical systems, such as air conditioning and ventilation, to create a comfortable environment for customers and staff. Additionally, we designed the electrical systems, including lighting and security systems, to ensure the branch's safety and functionality. Throughout the project, we collaborated closely with the Libyan Islamic Bank to understand their specific needs and preferences. Our team of experienced architects, engineers, and designers worked diligently to deliver a high-quality and functional branch that reflected the bank's brand identity. By leveraging our expertise in architectural design, mechanical engineering, and electrical engineering, we successfully contributed to the creation of a modern and efficient banking facility for Libyan Islamic Bank Alzahra branch.
