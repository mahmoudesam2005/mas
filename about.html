    The Mas Consultancy Company

    Mass Engineering Consultancy Company is a leading firm established in 2023, specializing in architectural design, structural engineering, and comprehensive engineering consultancy. With expertise in civil, electrical, and mechanical engineering, we offer a wide range of consulting services to meet diverse project needs.
    Architectural Design and Urban Planning: Our team excels in innovative design and urban planning, transforming concepts into reality. Whether it's residential, commercial, or administrative buildings, we bring creativity and expertise to every project.
    Civil and Structural Engineering: From robust architectural structures to end-to-end project management, we deliver exceptional civil and structural engineering solutions. Our focus on precision and quality ensures successful project execution.
    Electrical and Mechanical Engineering: With a deep understanding of electrical and mechanical systems, we design and implement efficient and reliable solutions. From air conditioning to lighting and security systems, our expertise guarantees optimal performance.
    Previous Projects: Our portfolio includes notable projects such as the rehabilitation and redesign of the Libyan Center for Oncology, the headquarters of the Libyan Islamic Bank, and various commercial and residential complexes. We have successfully executed projects across Tripoli, Benghazi, and other regions.
    Commitment to Quality: At Mass Engineering Consultancy Company, we prioritize excellence in all our endeavors. We leverage advanced engineering software, including AutoCAD, Photoshop, Reft24, and SketchUp, to deliver high-quality services. Our dedication to continuous improvement drives us to explore interior design services and aspire to engage in project management.
    Partners and Clients: We have established strong partnerships with esteemed organizations such as the Libyan Islamic Bank, Anwar Al Salam Hospital, and Nova Alpha Company. Additionally, we collaborate with renowned technical partners like Motto and Norm from Turkey, as well as an Egyptian office.
    Delivering Outstanding Consultancy Services: Our mission is to provide exceptional engineering consultancy services, consistently striving for excellence. With a focus on quality, innovation, and client satisfaction, Mass Engineering Consultancy Company is your trusted partner in achieving engineering success.
